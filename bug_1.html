Recon report 001

Confidential, Ministry of Truth eyes only


Date: Earth Year 2098 day 217

Type: Recon report

Designation: Cadet Zack Foreman

Location: Hellmire

As per ministry of truth request me and 3 other Helldivers embarked on a reconissance mission to Hellmire. Our mission was to ascertain activity of a new strain of Terminids in the area. Below is what we found...


As expected, a new strain was located and investigated. The Terminids displayed a capability to fly for an undetermined amount of time They did not display any ability to attack from a distance such as Bile Spewers. We have also located a new type of nest, specifically designed to house and deploy the flying terminids. After a brief evaluation of their movement patterns and strike tactics we have retreated to the LZ.


Unfortunately, during evac, two acompanying Helldivers were lost to the new strain of terminids.



C.O.R.A Recommendation: Suppression of information
